Offline Trial Tutorial

{% filter replace_terms %}

Run your trial offline, using our platform to randomise your participants and publish the results.

TRIAL DETAILS

Record your trial's title, description and what you'll be doing to the two groups.

TRIAL PARTICIPANTS

Upload a CSV containing a unique identifier for each of your participants in the first column.

The unique identifier must be comprised of letters, numbers and _.

PARTICIPANT RANDOMISATION

When you upload your participant list, we will randomise them, assigning each participant to their own group.

You can then download a CSV of your randomised participants.

UPLOAD RESULTS

After you've run your offline trial, you can upload the results as a CSV. Your CSV should contain three columns:

  1. Identifier
  2. Group
  3. result

If your variable is binary, 1 == yes, and 0 == no.

VIEW RESULTS

Once you've uploded your results, you can view the graphs on the site.
